About Us

Our hotel offers a perfect blend of comfort and practicality for today's discerning travelers. Conveniently located near the international airport, we provide an excellent choice for guests traveling for both business and leisure. The hotel's prime location also offers easy access to major business districts and cultural attractions.

We cater to a wide range of preferences and needs with our diverse room types. Choose from Standard Rooms, Twin Rooms, Executive Suites, Junior Presidential Suites, and Presidential Suites. Many rooms feature kitchenettes and refrigerators, perfect for extended stays, while others offer cozy, restful spaces for shorter visits.

Whether you're visiting for a brief stopover or an extended stay, our hotel seamlessly blends the convenience of premium hotel services with the comfort of a home-like atmosphere.
